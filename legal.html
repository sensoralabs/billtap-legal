BillTap is a billing and invoice management application designed for freelancers, shop owners, and small businesses.

8. Delete Account

Users can permanently delete their account and all associated data directly from the app.

How to delete account:

  • 1. Open the BillTap app
  • 2. Go to Settings
  • 3. Tap "Delete Account"
  • 4. Confirm deletion

What data is deleted:

  • User profile and account data
  • All invoices and business records (if synced)
  • Cloud-stored data (Firebase)

Data retention:

  • No personal data is retained after deletion
  • Temporary backups may persist for up to 30 days
