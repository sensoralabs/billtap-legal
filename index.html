8. Delete Account

Users can permanently delete their account and all associated data directly from the app.

How to delete account:

  • 1. Open the BillTap app
  • 2. Go to Settings
  • 3. Tap on "Delete Account"
  • 4. Confirm deletion

What data is deleted:

  • User profile (name, email, business details)
  • All invoices and billing data (if synced)
  • Cloud-stored data (Firebase)

Data retention:

  • No personal data is retained after deletion
  • Some data may remain temporarily in backups (up to 30 days) for security and recovery purposes
