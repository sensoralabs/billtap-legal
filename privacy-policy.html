Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

BillTap (“we”, “our”, or “us”) operates the BillTap mobile application. This page informs users about our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information when using our app.

1. Information We Collect

BillTap does not require user signup or login. We do not directly collect personal information such as name, email, phone number, or address.

2. Business Data

Any business details (such as business name, items, invoice amounts) entered by the user are stored locally on the user’s device and are not transmitted to our servers.

3. Payments

BillTap offers a premium subscription through Google Play Billing. All payment processing is handled securely by Google. We do not store or process any payment or card details.

4. Advertisements

BillTap uses Google AdMob to show advertisements to free users. AdMob may collect device-related data as per Google’s privacy policy.

Premium users may receive an ad-free experience.

5. Generated Documents

Invoices and PDFs generated in the app remain on the user’s device. When shared (for example via WhatsApp), the data is shared only according to the user’s action.

6. Children’s Privacy

BillTap is intended for general business use and is not designed specifically for children under the age of 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
